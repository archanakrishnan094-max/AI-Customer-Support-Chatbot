# 🤖 ROB – AI Service Discovery Chatbot

An interactive AI-powered chatbot built using **Landbot AI** to automate customer engagement and guide visitors through AI-powered business services.

The chatbot helps potential clients quickly understand available services, reduces repetitive customer inquiries, and provides an easy self-service experience before contacting the business.

# 📌 Project Overview

ROB is a conversational AI chatbot designed to introduce visitors to AI-based digital services through a guided conversation.

Instead of browsing multiple webpages, users simply interact with the chatbot, choose the service they need, and receive structured information instantly.

The chatbot acts as a virtual assistant that is available 24/7 to answer common customer questions and improve lead engagement.

---

# 🚩 Problem Statement

Many small businesses and freelancers lose potential clients because:

- Visitors cannot quickly understand available services.
- Businesses repeatedly answer the same customer questions.
- Website visitors leave without taking action.
- Manual customer support consumes valuable time.

A conversational chatbot provides an engaging way to introduce services while reducing manual effort.

---

# 💡 Solution

ROB automates the first interaction with website visitors by providing a guided conversation.

The chatbot:

- Welcomes users
- Understands what service they are interested in
- Displays available AI services
- Guides visitors through each service
- Improves customer experience
- Reduces repetitive manual conversations

# 🔄 Workflow

1. Visitor opens the chatbot.
2. Chatbot greets the visitor.
3. Visitor selects a service.
4. Chatbot provides relevant information.
5. Visitor explores additional services.
6. Visitor is guided toward contacting the business.
